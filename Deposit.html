Deposit Schemes

Compulsory Deposit Scheme

Every member has to make a Compulsory Deposit of Rs.200/- every month. An interest of 9% is paid against this deposit. It is compulsory for all members.

Recurring Deposit Scheme

Recurring Deposits allow members to save regularly with fixed monthly deposits over time. The interest rate is up to 9% P.A.

Fixed Deposit Scheme

A fixed deposit provides a higher interest rate until maturity. We offer deposits from 12 months up to 18 years.

Optional Deposit Scheme

This scheme allows members to deposit and withdraw funds anytime, providing more flexibility.
